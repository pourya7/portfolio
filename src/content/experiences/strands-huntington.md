## Huntington Bank at Strands

As part of the Strands team, I contributed to the development of an **AI-powered financial management tool** for Huntington Bank, one of the largest banks in the US Midwest. The project positioned Huntington Bank as a **digital leader** in the US banking sector.

### Key Achievements

- Developed front-end components for Huntington Bank's AI-powered financial management tool
- Improved **user engagement metrics** through enhanced UX design and implementation
- Helped position Huntington Bank as a digital banking leader through innovative features
- Delivered compliant, accessible UI meeting US banking regulatory requirements

### Technologies

- React, JavaScript
- AI-powered financial tools
- Enterprise banking integrations
- Accessibility-compliant UI (WCAG)
