## MyZFX Customer Portal at Zeal Tech

As **Area Product Owner** at Zeal Tech (London), I led the complete redevelopment and modernization of Zeal's primary customer portal, delivering measurable improvements in performance and user conversion.

### Key Achievements

- Re-architected the MyZFX web portal by implementing a **new design system**
- Cut **page-load times by 40%** and reduced **onboarding drop-offs by 30%**
- Streamlined global user onboarding by leading the integration of **SumSub for KYC/AML**
- Reduced verification wait times from **24 hours to 2 hours**
- Enhanced user experience and expanded product adoption in key regions

### Technologies

- React, TypeScript, Next.js
- Design system architecture
- Performance optimization
- KYC/AML integration
- Cross-regional product management
