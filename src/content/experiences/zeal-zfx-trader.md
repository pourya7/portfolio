## ZFX Trader Mobile App at Zeal Tech

As **Area Product Owner** at Zeal Tech (London), I delivered the company's first mobile trading application for **iOS and Android**, localized for MENA and LATAM markets.

### Key Achievements

- Delivered the company's **first mobile trading application** (iOS/Android)
- Localized web platforms for **MENA and LATAM** markets
- Scaled to **100k downloads** with a **50% week-one activation rate**
- Streamlined global user onboarding by leading the integration of **SumSub for KYC/AML**, reducing verification wait times from 24 hours to 2 hours
- Boosted **first-trade conversion by 18%** through optimized onboarding flows

### Technologies

- React Native (iOS/Android)
- Trading platform APIs
- KYC/AML integration (SumSub)
- User analytics and A/B testing
- Product management (Jira, Confluence)
